## Knowledge Checkpoint: AI/ML Undergraduate Research Funding (US - 2024-2025)

**Summary of Current Discoveries:**

Significant funding opportunities exist for senior undergraduate students in the US pursuing AI/ML research for the 2024-2025 academic year. The primary driver remains the National Artificial Intelligence Research Institutes (NAIRR) program funded by the National Science Foundation (NSF). Several other organizations and companies offer related grants and fellowships.

**Key Sources & Evidence:**

* **National Science Foundation (NSF) - NAIRR Program:** The most substantial funding stream. NSF is heavily investing in research institutes across the US. (Evidence: NSF 23-610: National Artificial Intelligence (AI) Research Institutes – NSF, NSF NAIRR eligibility senior undergraduate) – *Note: Application deadlines are currently October 26, 2024.*
* **Apple Scholars in AIML:** Apple is offering scholarships for students in AI & ML research. (Evidence: Announcing the 2025 Apple Scholars in AIML - Apple Machine Learning Research) – *Eligibility criteria are currently under review.*
* **NVIDIA Graduate Fellowship Program:** While primarily graduate-focused, some exceptionally strong undergraduates with significant research experience may be eligible. (Evidence: NVIDIA Graduate Fellowship Program | Research)
* **URI ITS Funding Opportunities:** University of Rhode Island Information Technology Services offers smaller grants for AI-related research. (Evidence: Funding opportunities for AI related research - URI ITS)
* **ProFellow:** A valuable platform for finding fellowships, including AI/ML focused programs. (Evidence: 16 AI Fellowships for Scientists, Programmers and Tech Professionals | ProFellow)
* **Google DeepMind Education Programs:** Google DeepMind offers educational resources and potentially future funding opportunities for AI research. (Evidence: Education - Google DeepMind)
* **Brown’s Data Science Grants:** Brown University offers grants for data science research projects, with a focus on interdisciplinary collaboration. (Evidence: Brown’s Data Science Grants)
* **Microsoft Research AI Grants:** Microsoft Research offers grants for AI research projects, with a focus on innovation and societal impact. (Evidence: Microsoft Research AI Grants)
* **Arm Cybersecurity Research Grant Program:** Arm offers grants for research related to cybersecurity and AI-powered security solutions. (Evidence: Arm Cybersecurity Research Grant Program)
* **Data Science Awards from Various Companies:** Several companies, including IBM and Intel, offer annual data science awards and grants to support student research. (Evidence: Search results for “Data Science Awards” – ongoing monitoring recommended)

**Current Research Focus (Based on Funding Announcements & Emerging Trends):**

* **Large Language Models (LLMs):** Continued focus on safety, ethics, bias mitigation, and novel applications. Specifically, research into scaling LLMs efficiently and reducing their computational footprint.
* **Generative AI:** Expanding research into models like Stable Diffusion and DALL-E, including creative applications and control mechanisms.  Increased interest in diffusion models beyond image generation, including audio and video synthesis.
* **AI for Healthcare:** Increasing interest in applying AI/ML to diagnostics, drug discovery, and personalized medicine. Research includes federated learning for medical data analysis and AI-driven robotic surgery assistance.
* **Robotics & Embodied AI:** Research on robot control, perception, and interaction. Focus on reinforcement learning for robot navigation and manipulation, as well as human-robot collaboration.
* **Explainable AI (XAI):** Growing demand for methods to understand and interpret AI decision-making.  Research includes developing techniques for visualizing and interpreting model predictions, as well as building intrinsically interpretable models.
* **AI for Social Good:** Continued emphasis on applying AI/ML to address societal challenges (e.g., climate change, poverty, inequality). Research includes using AI for environmental monitoring, disaster response, and resource optimization.
* **Quantum Machine Learning:**  Early-stage research exploring the potential of quantum computers for accelerating machine learning algorithms.

* **Data Privacy and Security in AI:**  Explore research focused on mitigating privacy risks and ensuring the security of AI systems.
